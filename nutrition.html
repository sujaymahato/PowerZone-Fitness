Nutrition FAQs

What are macronutrients?

Macronutrients are the nutrients required in large amounts in the diet, including proteins, fats, and carbohydrates.

How much water should I drink?

It is generally recommended to drink at least 8-10 cups of water a day, but individual needs may vary.

Do I need supplements?

Supplements can be helpful, but it's best to get your nutrients from a balanced diet first.
